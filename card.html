Red Fox

Red foxes can produce over 20 different sounds...

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes vulpes
  • Average Length: 39-43 inches long
  • Average Lifespan: 2-4 years
  • Habitat: Edges of forests, tilled fields, and near marshes

The largest of the true foxes and one of the most widely-distributed members of the canid species. They can be found across the entire Northern Hemisphere including most of North America, Europe and Asia, plus parts of North Africa.
